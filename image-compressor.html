How Our Image Compressor Works

Our advanced image compression algorithm analyzes your image and applies optimization techniques to reduce file size while maintaining visual quality:

For JPG Images:

  • Adjusts compression level (quality factor)
  • Optimizes Huffman tables
  • Removes unnecessary metadata (EXIF, etc.)
  • Applies chroma subsampling when beneficial

For PNG Images:

  • Reduces color palette when possible
  • Optimizes filter selection
  • Compresses with optimal DEFLATE parameters
  • Removes unnecessary chunks (text, etc.)

For WebP Images:

  • Adjusts quality/speed tradeoff
  • Optimizes encoding parameters
  • Removes metadata
  • Uses advanced compression techniques

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this tool free to use?

Yes, our image compressor is completely free to use with no hidden costs or watermarks.

Are my images secure?

Absolutely! All processing happens in your browser - your images never leave your device and are never stored on our servers.

What's the maximum file size?

You can compress images up to 20MB in size. For larger files, consider using professional desktop software.

Which format should I choose?

For photos: JPG or WebP. For graphics with transparency: PNG or WebP. WebP generally offers the best compression.

How much can I reduce file size?

Depending on the image and settings, you can typically reduce file size by 50-80% without noticeable quality loss.
