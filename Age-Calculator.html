About Our Age Calculator

Our free online age calculator calculates your exact age in years, months, and days. Whether you're curious about how old you are, need to know someone's age for official purposes, or just want to calculate the time between two dates, our tool provides accurate results instantly.

How to Use the Age Calculator

1. Enter your date of birth in the first field

2. Optionally, enter a target date to calculate your age on a specific date in the past or future

3. Click "Calculate Age" to see your exact age

Why Use Our Age Calculator?

- Precision: Calculates exact age down to the day

- Versatility: Works with any dates, past or future

- Comprehensive: Shows results in multiple formats (years, months, days, total days, etc.)

- Mobile-friendly: Works perfectly on all devices
